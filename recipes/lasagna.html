Lasagne

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish consisting of layers of flat pasta sheets filled with alternating layers of rich tomato sauce, creamy cheese, and savory meat, all baked to golden perfection.

It offers a harmonious blend of flavors and textures, creating a comforting and indulgent culinary experience.

Ingredients

  • lasagna noodles
  • ground beef
  • tomato sauce
  • ricotta cheese
  • eggs
  • fresh basil
  • olive oil

Steps

  1. Boil lasagna noodles.
  2. Brown ground beef (or sausage) with onions and garlic.
  3. Add tomato sauce to the meat mixture and simmer.
  4. Mix ricotta cheese with beaten eggs.
  5. Layer noodles, meat sauce, ricotta mixture, and mozzarella in a baking dish.
  6. Repeat the layers.
  7. Top with Parmesan cheese.
  8. Bake in the oven until bubbly and golden.
  9. Let it rest before serving.
  10. Enjoy delicious homemade lasagna!
